02 — Positioning

More than a
food destination.

Time Out Market Abu Dhabi isn't simply opening another food hall. Set within one of the UAE's most established cultural destinations, Saadiyat Island, it brings together the city's best chefs, creators and communities under one roof. Every piece of content should make audiences feel they're witnessing the making of Abu Dhabi's next cultural landmark, not simply watching a venue being marketed.

has a new spot.

03 — Pre-Opening · 4 Months · Anticipation

Early access,
before it exists.

Content moves
  • Chef Stories — meet the people behind every concept
  • The Sounds of… — ASMR-style sensory series per restaurant
  • First Taste Club — curated early-access invitation programme
  • The Chosen Ones — exclusive Instagram Broadcast Channel
  • Behind the Build — construction & menu development, revealed
Pre-Opening · Chef Stories

Meet the people
behind the food.

Short interviews exploring each chef's signature dish, inspirations and personality. Introduce the people before the food, and audiences start forming connections long before opening day.

Chef Stories — content example
Pre-Opening · The Sounds of…

Every kitchen has
its own soundtrack.

Every restaurant has its own identity, and every kitchen has its own soundtrack. Before opening, we'll visit each participating restaurant in its current location to capture the authentic sounds behind their signature dishes. These ASMR-style videos become a sensory introduction to every concept, building anticipation for their arrival at Time Out Market Abu Dhabi.

Pre-Opening · First Taste Club

Early access,
by invitation.

A curated digital invitation programme for food creators and local tastemakers. Selected guests become the first people invited to experience the market before it officially opens.

Pre-Opening · The Chosen Ones

The community
that gets there first.

An exclusive Instagram Broadcast Channel offering behind-the-scenes updates, sneak peeks and first-access announcements — building a community that feels involved in the journey, not just watching it unfold.

Pre-Opening · Behind the Build

Watch it
come to life.

From construction progress to menu development, audiences are invited behind the scenes to witness the creation of Time Out Market Abu Dhabi before it officially opens.

Pre-Opening · TikTok Strategy

Fast-paced,
platform-native.

TikTok focuses on fast-paced, platform-native storytelling rather than repurposed Instagram content — POVs, quick recommendations and playful food-first formats introducing audiences to what they'll soon be able to experience.

04 — Launch Month · Participation

Curiosity
becomes desire.

As opening approaches, focus shifts from introducing the market to showcasing everything that makes it unique — more immersive, revealing craftsmanship and atmosphere, inviting audiences into the conversation.

Content moves
  • Creator Collaboration Programme — long-term advocacy, not one-off posts
  • Community Activation — weekly competitions & interactive campaigns
  • Chef & Signature Dish Series — a moment for every concept
Launch Month · Creator Collaboration Programme

Advocacy,
not a one-off.

A long-term creator initiative that rewards ongoing advocacy instead of one-off campaigns. Early collaborators become ambassadors for the market, receiving exclusive opportunities and experiences throughout the year.

Launch Month · Community Activation

Give them a reason
to participate.

Weekly competitions and interactive campaigns keep audiences engaged throughout launch month — giveaways, exclusive experiences, voting mechanics and creator-led challenges building excitement ahead of opening.

every concept.

06 — Launch Day · Participation

Impossible
to miss.

Capturing the city's biggest food moment

Launch day content focuses on real-time storytelling — genuine reactions, packed spaces, and the atmosphere that makes audiences want to experience it for themselves.

Content moves
  • Live Coverage — continuous coverage across Stories, Reels and TikTok, from first guests through evening celebrations
  • Creator Activation — a select group experiences the market from different perspectives, driving awareness beyond launch day
  • User Generated Content — guests become part of the storytelling through reposts and interactive moments
07 — Post-Opening · 8–9 Months · Continuation

From launch moment
to always-on.

Opening day is only the beginning. The strategy evolves into building repeat visits, celebrating the community, and positioning TOMAD as an always-changing destination with something new to discover.

Content moves
  • Build Your Perfect Menu — creators curate a 3-course meal across concepts
  • Ongoing Creator Programme — weekly collaborations, not concentrated at launch
  • Time Out Market Creator Festival — a community film competition
  • Entertainment & Live Culture — music & weekend programming as an ongoing pillar
Post-Opening · TikTok Strategy

Community · Broadcast Channel

The first
to know.

The TOMAD Broadcast Channel becomes the brand's VIP community — giving followers exclusive access to updates they won't find anywhere else. From behind-the-scenes content and chef sneak peeks to launch announcements, giveaways and early event access, members become part of the journey before the market officially opens.

What members receive
  • Exclusive behind-the-scenes content from the build-up to opening
  • First look at new restaurant announcements and menu reveals
  • Early access to launches, events and ticketed experiences
  • Surprise giveaways, invitations and community rewards
  • Polls, Q&As and direct conversations that shape future content
10 — KPI & Reporting Framework

How we
measure it.

RFP baseline targets — adjusted post-signing against confirmed followers & market trends.

Platform Growth

  • 10,000 Instagram followers — pre-opening
  • 5,000 TikTok followers — pre-opening
  • 3–5 Reels/TikToks per month exceeding 10K views

Engagement

  • 3–5% average IG engagement rate (organic)
  • 10+ second average watch time — TikTok & Reels

Influencer

  • 15 partnerships activated, 15+ posts minimum
  • 2–3x account average reach per influencer post
  • 80%+ of posts include CTA + tagged location

Traffic & Conversion

  • 10%+ QoQ increase in clicks to site / reservations
  • Trackable redemptions — QR codes, promos, events
  • 4:1 minimum ROI — reservations & groups PPC
